# Telegram

I build Telegram bots — that's the core of what I do here. If you already have a userbot and need something built on top of it (a parser, for example), I'm open to that too, but userbots themselves aren't something I build from scratch.

Bots I build tend to be functional rather than decorative: automation, data collection, notifications, integrations with a larger backend — not just a menu of buttons.
